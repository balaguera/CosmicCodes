◆ TFset_parameters()

void PowerSpectrum::TFset_parameters ( )

Setting variables for Fitting Formula Eisenstein and Hu.

Set all the scalars quantities for Eisenstein & Hu 1997 fitting formula /*

Parameters
omega0hhThe density of CDM and baryons, in units of critical dens, multiplied by the square of the Hubble constant, in units of 100 km/s/Mpc /*
f_baryonThe fraction of baryons to CDM /*
TcmbThe temperature of the CMB in Kelvin. Tcmb<=0 forces use of the COBE value of 2.728 K. /*
Warning
Units are always Mpc, never h^-1 Mpc.
